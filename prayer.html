The Lord's Prayer

Our Father in heaven,
hallowed be your name,
Your kingdom come,
your will be done, on earth as it is in heaven.
Give us this day our daily bread,
and forgive us our debts, as we also have forgiven our debtors.
And lead us not into temptation,
but deliver us from evil.
For yours is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, forever. Amen.
Yaa Abbaa keenya, Waaqa irra kan jiraattu,
maqaan kee haa qulqullaa'u.
Mootummaan kee haa dhufu,
jaalalli kee waaqa irratti akka ta'e, akkasumas lafa irratti haa ta'u.
Kan nu ga'u buddena keenya har'a nuuf kenni,
yakka keenyas nuuf dhiisi, nus kan nu yakkaniif akkuma dhiifnu.
Qorumsatti nu hin galchin,
hamaa nu oolchi malee.
Mootummaan kan keetii'oo, humnis, galatnnis, bara baraan. Ameen!
