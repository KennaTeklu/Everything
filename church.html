About Sunday School Attendance Manager

Version 2.0.0 – Fully functional release

Features

  • Student, teacher & parent registration with unique IDs
  • Attendance tracking with per-student history
  • Search & filter students by name, gender, and age group
  • Export data as CSV, JSON, or printable PDF
  • Full student profile view with attendance history
  • Customizable color themes and background
  • Voice recording for name pronunciation
  • Profile photo support
  • Photo gallery with tagging
  • Learning levels (mastered, learning, future)
  • Data backup & restore
  • All data stored locally in your browser
